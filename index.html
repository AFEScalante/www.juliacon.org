The third Julia conference will take place June 21st-25th, 2016 at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Expect cutting-edge technical talks, hands-on workshops, a chance to rub shoulders with Julia's creators, and a weekend in a city known for its historical significance and colonial architecture. Looking forward to seeing you there!

Registration closes on June 10, but be sure to buy your tickets by May 6 to take advantage of the early registration discount!


Get the official poster for the conference here!

Talk Submissions

Want to share your work with the Julia community? We'd love to hear from you! If you're interested in giving a talk, you can read the Call For Proposals for more details.

You can use this form to submit a proposal. The submission deadline is March 18th at 11:59pm SST. Proposal acceptance notifications will be sent out near the beginning of April.
