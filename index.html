The second Julia conference will take place June 24th-28th (Wednesday-Saturday) at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in Boston, Massachusetts. Expect two days of cutting-edge technical talks, a chance to rub shoulders with Julia's creators, and a weekend in a city known for its historical signicance and colonial architecture.

Program Committee

The JuliaCon program committee is composed of entirely of volunteer organizers and can be reached at juliacon@googlegroups.com with any questions or comments.
