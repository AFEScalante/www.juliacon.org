Accessibility at JuliaCon

JuliaCon is committed to providing an accessible and inclusive environment to every attendee. In light of our announcement to shift JuliaCon online, we shall ensure that all our talks will include automated closed captioning. Please reach out to Ranjan Anantharaman, the Accessibility Chair at juliacon@julialang.org for any additional concerns.
